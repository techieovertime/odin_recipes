Pizza

This is a simple recipe to make an unnamed famous orange cat's favorite pepperoni with cheese pizza.

Ingredients

  • 1 13.8oz Pillsbury Classic Pizza Crust
  • 1 8oz Kraft Mozzarella Shredded Cheese
  • 1/8cup Prego Pizza Sauce
  • 25 slices Pepperoni (any brand)

Steps

  1. Open & roll out the can of Pillsbury Classic Pizza Crust onto a 9"x12" sheet pan
  2. Spread 1/8cup of Prego pizza sauce evenly onto the dough
  3. Sprinkle 8oz Kraft Mozzarella Shredded Cheese evenly on the dough
  4. Spead 25 slices of pepperoni evenly onto the dough
  5. Bake pizza for 15 minutes at 400 degrees Farenheit
  6. Cool pizza for 10 minutes, slice, & share!

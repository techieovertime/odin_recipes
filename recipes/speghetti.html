Speghetti

This is a simple recipe to make an unnamed famous orange cat's favorite speghetti with meat sauce.

Ingredients

  • 1 16oz Barilla Spaghetti
  • 1 45oz Prego Pasta Sauce with Meat Flavor

Steps

  1. Prepare 16oz Barilla Spaghetti according to instructions
  2. Strain the spaghetti & set aside
  3. Stir 45oz Prego Pasta Sauce with Meat Flavor on medium heat for 15 minutes
  4. Add cooked spaghetti to pasta sauce, stir, & share!
